How it works

Streets are grouped into three tiers: major, secondary, and residential. Master the major roads first before unlocking the next tier.

The quiz uses spaced repetition (SRS) — streets you struggle with appear more often. Streets you know well are spaced further apart.

Multiple choice is used for major and secondary roads. Type-in mode unlocks at the residential level.
